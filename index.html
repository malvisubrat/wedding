Schedule

21st July
Haldi & Mehndi Day
  • Haldi Ceremony: Morning ritual where turmeric paste is applied to the bride and groom for blessings and purification.
  • Mehndi Ceremony: Afternoon event where intricate henna designs are applied to the bride's hands and feet, symbolizing joy and beauty.
22nd July
Wedding Ceremony & Vidai
  • 13:00 - Lunch: A family meal shared with guests, marking the beginning of the wedding celebrations for the day.
  • 15:00 - Wedding Ceremony: Traditional rituals and vows exchanged in a beautiful outdoor setting, followed by a celebratory procession.
  • Vidai: A heartfelt farewell to the bride as she departs from her parental home to start her new life with the groom.
24th July
Reception Day in Kumurisingha village
  • 13:00 - Reception Ceremony: A formal event where the newly married couple is introduced to extended family, friends, and guests.
